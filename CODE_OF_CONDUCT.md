# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

Contributors and maintainers are expected to provide a respectful, harassment-free environment for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Focus on technical issues, not personal attacks.
- Assume good intent and ask clarifying questions.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing private or sensitive information without consent.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, reject contributions, or restrict participation for violations.
